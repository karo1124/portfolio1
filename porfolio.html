3 SEMESTER - ABROAD IN CANADA

In my third semester, I intentionally chose to participate in an exchange program to enrich both my academic knowledge and cultural understanding. During this period, I enrolled in six courses, each contributing significantly to expanding my comprehension of the design field. Below, I highlight noteworthy achievements from specific projects within each course.

Print production

RECYCLED LOGO DESIGN

In the area of print production, I took on the job of creating a business-focused and eco-friendly logo for a tote bag. Following specific color guidelines, I came up with this result and made the design using Adobe Photoshop.

Editorial Design

AD FOR A MAGAZINE

In the Editorial Design course, the final project included making a 16-page magazine. I designed a two-page ad focusing on fashion, aiming for a stylish and clean look. The entire magazine was carefully put together using Adobe InDesign.

Web Design 2

DESIGN SYSTEM FILE

One of the assignments we had for this course, was to create a design system file in adobe XD. This way, you can ensure yourself to always use UI and UX design consistently for you or for current team members. This assignments is made in adobe XD.

Illustrator Workshop

BOOK COVER

For this course, we had to make a lot of illustrations in adobe illustrater. For one of the last assignments, we had to make a bookcover for a book by our own choosing. I chose to work with shakespear - Hamlet, as i felt like i could express the subject of the book more clearly. This is made in adobe illustrator.

Intro/ Advertising & Copywriting

AD FOR TORONTO ZOO

This course was all about the advertising & copywriting - world. The picture i have chosen to show here, is from one of the last assignments, were we had to make an ad for Toronto Zoo. I chose to make it with a little bit of humor, so the ad would feel more reladable. The assignment was created in prohotoshop.
