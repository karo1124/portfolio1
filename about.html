ABOUT ME

Greeting!

I'm Karoline Thomasen, a 24-year-old graphic designer with a deep passion for infusing creativity into the digital world. My hobbies includes, painting, drawing, and knitting helps bring more creativity into imagination and shape my design ideas in my work. As i am a finished educated multimedia designer, I am committed to invest my heart and soul into every project i make. My attention to detail and my hard work in trying to balance between aesthetics and functionality, I allow myself to breathe life into logos, illustrations, and user-friendly interfaces.
I prioritize a collaborative and inclusive work environment, valuing the diverse input and perspectives that contribute to finding the best selution to a project. Driven by the desire to create visually compelling designs that endure in memory, I invite you to join me in bringing your vision to life.
